The project uses federal contract data to identify companies that may benefit from Colaberryâ€™s services. The dataset includes details about awarded contracts, enabling the system to:

  • Analyze patterns and detect opportunities

Data Sources

  • SAM.gov: Federal contractor profiles, including NAICS codes and business capabilities
  • Colaberry Internal Data: Existing partnerships and services to improve model recommendations



Key Data Fields

  • Award Number: Unique identifier for each contract
  • Awardee: Name of the company receiving the contract
  • Department/Agency: Federal entity awarding the contract
  • NAICS Code: Industry classification for service relevance
  • Award Date: Used to identify recent opportunities
  • Contract Description: Machine learning-analyzed text to determine relevance to data services 



Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

  • Accuracy of Recommendations: Percentage of recommended companies that show interest
  • Conversion Rate: Number of successful partnerships
  • Job Opportunities Created: New jobs generated through partnerships
  • Intern-to-Full-Time Conversion Rate: Percentage of interns transitioning to full-time roles
  • Outreach Efficiency: Time saved compared to manual methods
  • Trend Forecasting Accuracy: Accuracy in predicting federal contracting trends

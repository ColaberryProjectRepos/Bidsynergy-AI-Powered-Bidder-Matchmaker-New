Project Overview 1

Project Overview

Colaberry is developing a recommendation system to identify companies winning federal contracts that may require data contracting services. The system aims to foster partnerships and create job opportunities by matching Colaberry’s offerings with high-potential companies. Using collaborative filtering and Predictive Analytics, the solution will analyze contract data, such as award information and descriptions, to recommend companies for outreach.

Executive Summary

Colaberry’s initiative leverages advanced analytics to unlock new business opportunities by identifying federal contract winners in need of data services. Through collaborative filtering and NLP, the recommendation system matches awardees with Colaberry’s offerings, empowering the business development team for targeted outreach.


Background

The project will navigate complex federal contract data through collaborative filtering and NLP. This will enable Colaberry to streamline outreach, build strategic partnerships, and create job opportunities.

Additionally, the project offers hands-on experience to interns working with real-world data challenges, creating a talent pipeline to support Colaberry’s growth. It also forecasts federal contracting trends, ensuring proactive strategic alignment.


Problem Statement

Colaberry aims to identify federal contract-winning companies that require data services. The challenge lies in efficiently analyzing complex contract data to unlock business potential while fostering job creation and talent development within the organization.



Objective

The primary objective is to develop a data-driven recommendation system that identifies federal contract-winning companies likely to need data contracting services. This solution will:

  • Streamline outreach and foster partnerships
  • Create job opportunities and develop talent
  • Provide hands-on experience for Colaberry interns
  • Support long-term growth through trend forecasting
